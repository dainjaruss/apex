## 4. How to Stress Test Summary Groups & Forced Distribution

1. **Test Forced Distribution Quotas:**
   - Log in as `co.enterprise@franklyn.dev` (Reporting Senior) using password `NavyEval!2026`.
   - Navigate to **Summary Groups** in the top navigation bar.
   - Click into **IT1 Regular EVAL Summary Group (USS ENTERPRISE)**.
   - You will immediately see all **4 IT1 evaluations** attached to this group:
     - 1 Early Promote (EP) — 25% of group (compliant with EP quota)
     - 1 Must Promote (MP) — 50% EP+MP (compliant with 60% combined quota)
     - 2 Promotables (P)
   - Observe the real-time calculation of **Block 50a Summary Group Average** pooled across all 4 peers!

2. **Test Multi-Hop Custody Routing:**
   - Log in as `rater.it@franklyn.dev`.
   - Open one of the evaluations currently in Rater review (e.g., `CROSS, BENJAMIN B (STRESS #3)`).
   - Review Block 43 narrative bullets, check Block 41 career recommendations (capped at 2), and click **Approve & Route Forward** to send custody to the Senior Rater (`senior.rater1@franklyn.dev`).

3. **Test CO Digital Signature & Locking:**
   - Log in as `co.enterprise@franklyn.dev`.
   - Open an evaluation waiting in Reporting Senior review (e.g., `REYNOLDS, JOHN D (STRESS #1)`).
   - Scroll down to Block 50 (Reporting Senior Signature), use the interactive HTML5 canvas pad to sign, and complete sign-off.
   - Verify that the evaluation status transitions to `completed`, `signature_locked` becomes `true`, and the form becomes strictly read-only!
